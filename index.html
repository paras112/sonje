Run-On Sentence Rules:

A run-on sentence occurs when two sentences are joined with no punctuation or conjunction (and, but, or, nor, for, so, yet). Fix a run-on sentence by placing the correct end punctuation mark at the end of one sentence and capitalizing the first word in the next sentence.
